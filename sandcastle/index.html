How to play

Click and drag on the sand to place towers and walls or dig trenches. Waves arrive from the left; guide the water into your trenches and away from your fortifications. Press the reset button or hit R to start fresh.

  • 1: Tower   2: Wall   3: Trench   P: Pool (shallow radius 3)
  • Hold and drag to paint the selected tool.
  • Watch the countdown for the next wave.
